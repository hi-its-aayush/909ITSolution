FAQ

Common questions,
answered honestly

No jargon. No runaround. If you have a question not listed here, just call us.

What is included in managed IT services?
Our managed IT service includes 24/7 help desk support, proactive monitoring, patch and update management, network management, backup and disaster recovery, vendor management, and monthly reporting. Everything your business needs to run smoothly without an internal IT team.
How fast do you respond to IT issues?
For Priority 1 (business-critical outages), our average initial response is under 2 hours. Priority 2 issues are typically addressed within 4 business hours, and general requests within 1 business day. Managed clients also have access to after-hours emergency support.
Do you lock clients into long-term contracts?
No. We believe in earning your business every month — not locking you in. Our managed services run on monthly agreements and project engagements are fixed-price. We are confident enough in our service that we don't need contract clauses to keep clients.
Do you support businesses outside Sydney?
Yes. We have offices in Parramatta (Sydney) and Canberra, and support clients across Australia through remote delivery. For on-site work we cover Sydney and Canberra directly and coordinate trusted partners elsewhere.
What makes 909 IT different from other MSPs?
Three things: certified depth in Microsoft and Fortinet, a genuine no-lock-in model, and we measure success by your outcomes not our ticket volume. After 10+ years and 1000+ clients, our repeat business rate tells the story better than we can.
Can you migrate us from our current provider?
Absolutely. We handle provider transitions regularly — documentation handover, system access transfer, and technical migration. Most clients experience zero downtime during the switch. We also offer a free assessment first so you know exactly what you are moving to.
