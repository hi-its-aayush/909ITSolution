Our Story

A decade of delivering IT that actually works

909 IT Solutions was founded with a straightforward belief: businesses deserve IT support that is genuinely proactive, not just reactive. Too many organisations were stuck in a break-fix cycle — paying for IT when things went wrong instead of preventing problems in the first place.

Over 10 years, that philosophy has driven everything we do. We built our team around certified engineers, not generalists. We chose to go deep on Microsoft and Fortinet rather than claim expertise in everything. And we made a commitment to transparency — no lock-in contracts, no hidden fees, no jargon.

Today, with offices in Parramatta and Canberra, we support businesses of all sizes across Australia — from single-site GP clinics to multi-location enterprises. The team has grown, the technology has changed, but the philosophy hasn’t.

“We grow alongside our clients by understanding their needs and requirements — not by locking them into a box.”

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

These aren’t poster values. They are the principles we use to make decisions every day.

Our Mission

To add genuine value to every client we work with. At 909 IT, we convert technology challenges into solutions that work in the real world — without compromising on security and without overcomplicating the simple. Our mission is to deliver quality outcomes, every time.

Our Vision

To be Australia’s most trusted technology partner for growing businesses — not the biggest, but the best. We envision a future where every Australian business has access to enterprise-grade IT expertise, regardless of their size, at a price that makes sense.

Our Values

Clients first, always. Certified expertise, not claimed expertise. Honest communication — we tell you what you need to hear. Long-term thinking over quick fixes. And accountability: we own our outcomes, good or bad, and use every experience to get better.
